Overview of the works of the robot

Mobile Base: The robot will take in Lidar data which will be passed to AMCL for localization of the robot and SLAM for mapping of the area.

Arm Manipulator: The robot will take in BGR and depth data through the RGB and depth cameras. The data will be passed to OpenCV to identify the object and segmenter to crop out the image. The information will then be use to generate a goal.

The output from the AMCL(Mobile base) and the goal(Arm manipulator) will be given to Moveit to perform path planning.

Output from Moveit will be sent to the drivers of the robot to perform the physical tasks.
